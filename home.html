Assosciation of Women in Computing - Puget Sound Chapter

The Association for Women in Computing (AWC) is a national, nonprofit, professional organization for individuals with an interest in information technology. The purpose of the Puget Sound Chapter is to:

  • Promote awareness on issues affecting women in the computing industry.
  • Further the professional development and advancement of women in computing.
  • Encourage women to enter computing as a career.
